About

There are two key developments in the agricultural world - The issues of Food Security and Food Quality.

Access to sufficient food at affordable prices will become problematic over the next 30 to 40 years as the global population surges towards 9 billion.

We have also observed significant changes in community attitudes towards food. People are demanding to eat fresh, safe and reliable foods.

Australia is ideally placed to take advantage of these important global trends. It is blessed with vast farming lands covering diverse climatic zones. The country has a strong reputation for producing premium produce across a wide variety of products including dairy, beef, grains, wine, fruit & vegetables.

The emergence of Asia provides Australia with an opportunity to rapidly grow its agricultural sector. However, this will require a substantial injection of capital from both Australian and overseas investors.

The challenge is gaining a foothold in such a massive, diverse and complex marketplace.

Drover Group has extensive experience and networks across Australia and Asia. We are experienced in both the Eastern and Western styles of business which makes us ideally placed to help your brand cut through the red tape, make the right contacts and establish the relationships that leads to long-terms success.

We have formed successful partnerships with Australian premium producers of fresh fruits and vegetables, beef, milk, wine and juices. We're also able to source product on demand through our network.
